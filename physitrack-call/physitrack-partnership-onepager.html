StrydeOS × Physitrack

We surface your engine.
We don't replace it.

StrydeOS is the operating layer above the PMS for UK private physiotherapy. We do not build exercise content, telehealth, or triage. We pull Physitrack adherence and outcomes into one clinic view, and trigger programme assignment automatically off PMS events so clinicians stop double-keying and patients stop falling through the gaps. Every clinic connects its own paid Physitrack account. The net effect for Physitrack: more programmes assigned, deeper utilisation, stickier accounts.

01 / DISTRIBUTION

A channel, not a reseller

Every clinic we onboard authorises its own Physitrack subscription. Clinics not yet on Physitrack are net-new seats we route to you.

02 / UTILISATION

We drive programmes assigned

Our entire reason to exist is lifting HEP compliance. A higher assignment rate is, directly, more Physitrack programmes prescribed per clinic.

03 / RETENTION

Stickier clinic accounts

When Physitrack data is wired into the owner's KPI view, it becomes load-bearing. Embedded data is the deepest churn moat there is.

We dogfood at Spires Physiotherapy, West Hampstead, on Physitrack + WriteUpp. These are our live numbers, and the gap we close is the volume you gain.
