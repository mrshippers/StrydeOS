## Tone Guide — Quick Reference

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Confident, earned swagger | Arrogant, punching down |
| Data-first, then the punchline | Leading with hype |
| Cheeky, lightly provocative | Crude, sarcastic, dismissive |
| UK English, clinic-owner vocabulary | Generic SaaS speak, Americanisms |
| Collaborative on Ava ("reinforced", "got it") | Aggressive ("fire", "replace", "ditch") |
| Specific numbers, real comparisons | Vague percentages, "up to" claims |
| Peer-to-peer with owners | Talking down or hand-holding |
