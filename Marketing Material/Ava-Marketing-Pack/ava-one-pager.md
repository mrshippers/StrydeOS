# Ava — AI Receptionist for Private Practice

## The Problem
Your front desk is a bottleneck. Missed calls = missed revenue. Answering services take messages but don't book. After hours, you're invisible.

## The Solution
Ava is an AI voice receptionist that picks up every call, books appointments directly into your PMS, and recovers cancellations — 24/7.

## How it works
1. Patient calls your clinic number
2. Ava answers in under 3 seconds — natural, conversational voice
3. Identifies the request (new booking, reschedule, cancellation, query)
4. Books directly into WriteUpp, Cliniko, or your PMS of choice
5. Logs the call — duration, outcome, booking status — into your StrydeOS dashboard
