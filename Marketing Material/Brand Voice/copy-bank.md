## Tone Quick Reference

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Specific numbers, real comparisons | Vague percentages, "up to" claims |
| Trailing implications — let the reader land | Spelling out the punchline |
| UK cities — Sheffield, Bristol, Manchester, Clapham | Generic "a local practice" |
| Earned confidence | Performed enthusiasm |
| Collaborative on Ava | Any anti-worker framing |
| Data → implication | Implication → data |
| Two-beat rhythm over three | Rule of three |
| "Would Jamal send this to Joe" | Anything that sounds like it was assembled |
